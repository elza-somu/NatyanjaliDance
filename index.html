Natyanjali Dance Academy

Kalakshetra Style

At Natyanjali, the emphasis is on mastering the fundamentals of Bharatanatyam, including body posture, footwork, hand gestures, facial expressions, and rhythm. Students also learn about the history and culture of Bharatanatyam, as well as the meaning and significance of the different dance pieces they perform.

The academy offers classes for students of all ages and levels, from beginners to advanced dancers. Classes are conducted in small groups to ensure individual attention and personalized feedback. The academy also provides opportunities for students to perform on stage, both within the academy and at various cultural events and festivals.

Artistic Director : Sukanya Ajith

A professional classical dancer with a Post Graduate in Bharathanatyam from Kalakshetra Chennai.

She was born and brought up in Idukki, Kerala. She started practicing classical dance such as Bharathanatyam, Mohiniyattam and Kuchipudi in her very young age and had been performing in various stages in Kerala. After high school, she moved to Chennai for her Higher studies in Bharathanatyam at Kalakshetra foundation.

Kalakshetra troop is famous across the globe for stage performance and she was one of the key performer in the troop until she moved to Canada. Her performances in Iskcon (Bangalore), Suva&Nadi (Fiji) are some in many with Kalakshetra troop.
